## Mathematical Notation

**∇**: Gradient (nabla)  
**∂**: Partial derivative  
**Σ**: Summation  
**∏**: Product  
**∈**: Element of  
**⊆**: Subset of  
**∀**: For all  
**∃**: There exists  
**arg max**: Argument of the maximum  
**arg min**: Argument of the minimum  
**s.t.**: Subject to (in optimization)  
**w.r.t.**: With respect to
